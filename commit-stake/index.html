How CommitStake works

1
Stake. You lock USDC behind a goal, naming a verifier (who judges it) and a beneficiary (who gets the funds if you fail).
2
Resolve. Before the deadline the verifier marks pass (stake unlocked for you) or fail (stake slashed to the beneficiary).
3
Claim or slash. On pass, you claim your stake back. If the verifier stays silent past the deadline, anyone can trigger the slash — they can't grief you by going quiet.

No owner, no admin keys, no protocol fee. It's a public building block — language tests, fitness goals, code challenges, accountability bets. Built on Arc, where USDC is the gas token.
