Three composable, owner-less USDC primitives for autonomous agents and SME commerce — deployed and live on the Arc testnet. Each shares one design: no admin keys, balance-delta custody, USDC as both the rail and the gas.

StreamPay

Continuous USDC payment streams — pay an agent by the second, meter an API by usage, settle subscriptions and vesting in real time. Withdraw what's streamed anytime; cancel splits the rest fairly.

AgentBond

Reputation-backed credit for agents. An agent posts a USDC bond and grants protocols the right to slash it — the shared enforcement layer beneath uncollateralized agent credit, marketplaces, and on-chain insurance.

CommitStake

Milestone-based USDC escrow. Lock a stake behind a commitment; a verifier marks pass or fail before the deadline. Succeed and reclaim it, fail or expire and it's slashed to the beneficiary.
